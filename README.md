# Kohinoor Gems & Jewellers - Luxury Gold, Silver & Gemstone Jewellery Website



Custom WordPress website developed for Kohinoor Gems & Jewellers, a trusted jewellery showroom offering BIS-hallmarked gold, 925 sterling silver, certified gemstones, premium pearls, and custom heritage designs in Udaipur, Rajasthan (2025 client project).

## Project Overview
- **Client Industry**: Jewellery Retail & Custom Craftsmanship (Gold, Silver, Gemstones, Pearls, Jadav Work, Bespoke Pieces)
- **My Role**: Full custom WordPress development, theme customization, elegant product layouts, performance optimization
- **Timeline**: 4-6 weeks
- **Team Size**: Solo developer
- **Live Website**: [https://kohinoorgemsandjewellers.com/](https://kohinoorgemsandjewellers.com/)

## Key Features Implemented
- Hero section with emotional tagline "Jewellery That Shines With Your Story" and emphasis on crafting timeless elegance, purity, and trust
- Featured products/services showcase: Gold Jewellery on Order, 925 Silver Jadav Jewellery, Silver Jewellery for Her, Certified Natural Gemstones (Sapphire, Emerald, Ruby), Premium Pearls, Heritage Jadav Work, Custom/Bespoke Designs
- About section highlighting blending traditional craftsmanship with contemporary designs, finest material sourcing, authenticity (BIS hallmarked), customer trust, affordable luxury, unmatched quality, and legacy of happy customers
- "Why Choose Us" highlights: Unmatched Craftsmanship, Authenticity Guaranteed, Exclusive Collections, Natural Gemstones & Pearls (healing value), Tailored Creations, Trusted Legacy
- Testimonials carousel with real client quotes praising quality, design, service, packaging, and satisfaction
- FAQ section covering hallmarking, purity, custom orders, Jadav jewellery, and certifications
- Responsive, luxurious design optimized for jewellery visuals (mobile-first)
- SEO-friendly for local Udaipur jewellery/gemstone searches

## Technical Stack
- WordPress v6.7+
- PHP 8.2+
- Elementor Pro (or similar builder) + custom CSS for gold/luxury styling, product grids, and elegant aesthetics
- JavaScript for testimonials slider, smooth interactions
- Plugins: Elementor, contact forms (WPForms or similar), Yoast SEO, image optimization/cache
- Performance: WebP images, lazy loading for high-res jewellery photos
